# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This is a small, friendly hobby project. Everyone interacting in the project's
issues, pull requests, and other spaces is expected to be respectful and
considerate.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind, patient, and welcoming.
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory remarks of any kind.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the
maintainer at <duetscher@jam-software.com>. Reports will be handled
confidentially. The maintainer may remove comments, commits, or contributors
that violate this Code of Conduct.

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
